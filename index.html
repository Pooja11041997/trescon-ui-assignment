What Is Trescon Virtual?

An extension of Trescon’s growing catalogue of events services, Trescon Virtual is a line of digital related events created for industry leaders to explore and share their knowledge, insights and experiences on the most pressing issues that are relevant towards business requirements and its overall success.


Curated for more smaller scale and scope, the event may be conducted over the course of a few hours over multiple days depending on the agenda’s requirement using the most advanced online conferencing technologies, tailor made to suit your business requirements.
